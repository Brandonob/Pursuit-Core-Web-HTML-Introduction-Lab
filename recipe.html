The Perfect PB&J

Description

This peanut butter sndwhich is my favorite sandwhich. It has the perfect balance of ingrdients and looks great when made right.

Ingredients

  • 1 jar of grape jelly

  • A cutting board

  2. 1/8 of PB on left side

  5. cut sandwhich in half
